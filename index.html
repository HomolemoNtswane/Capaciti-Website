About CAPACITI

Our Mission

CAPACITI is dedicated to closing the digital skills gap in Africa by providing underserved youth with access to world-class tech education, mentorship, and employment opportunities.

Our Vision

We envision an Africa where every young person has the opportunity to participate in and contribute to the digital economy, driving innovation and economic growth across the continent.

Our History

2018

CAPACITI founded as a division of UVU Africa with initial funding from key partners.

2019

Launched first cohort with 50 students in Web Development and Data Science tracks.

2020

Pivoted to virtual learning during pandemic, expanding reach across South Africa.

2021

Graduated 500+ students with 85% placement rate into tech roles.

2022

Expanded programs to include Cybersecurity and Cloud Computing tracks.

2023

Launched initiatives in 3 additional African countries with corporate partners.
